Why Notary is unique?

Notary is aiming to provide enterprise-grade solutions and cross-industry standards for securing software supply chain

Cryptographic Signing

We support COSE and JWS signatures for secure authentication, enabling versatile signing and verification of software artifacts. Built on standard PKI, our system ensures reliable and compatible cryptographic operations, accommodating online and air-gapped signing scenarios for diverse requirements.

Security Policy

We offer the ability to customize trust policies, allowing users to determine the authenticity of signed artifacts. We ensure that all artifacts are signed with trusted identities and originate from trusted registries, enhancing system integrity and authenticity.

Extensibility

Our system provides extensibility options that allow users to customize and adapt the platform according to their specific needs. With a flexible architecture and robust APIs, you can integrate and extend functionalities to meet your requirements effectively.

Multi-registry

We support multiple registries, enabling you to manage and organize artifacts across different repositories. Our platform provides seamless integration and synchronization capabilities, allowing you to efficiently work with artifacts from various sources.

Community-driven

Our platform is community-driven, with active participation and collaboration from developers worldwide. Join our vibrant community to share knowledge, contribute to the ecosystem, and benefit from collective expertise to drive innovation and growth.
